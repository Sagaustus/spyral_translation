Models and benchmarks

  • NLLB-200 for multilingual machine translation drafts.
  • XLM-R sentence embeddings (via sentence-transformers) for semantic similarity / back-translation checks.
  • OpenAI (optional) for alternative draft generation and comparison.
  • Ollama (optional) for local, pluggable LLM experimentation.
  • AfroLingu-MT benchmark dataset for evaluating translation quality on African language pairs.
