How strings are counted

Approved

Human reviewers have accepted the AI draft (or edited it) and marked it as ready for export. These strings drive the completion percentage.

In review

A reviewer has opened the string and is currently working on it. Temporarily locked from other reviewers.

Machine draft

AI translation generated but not yet touched by a human reviewer. May include QA flags if automated checks detected issues.
