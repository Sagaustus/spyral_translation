What the columns mean

AI Translated

Total strings where AI has produced a draft translation, regardless of review status.

Approved

Human-validated strings, export-ready. Drives the % progress bar.

Awaiting Review

AI draft exists but no human has reviewed it yet. Click Review to start.

In Review

A reviewer has submitted text and it is waiting for final approver sign-off.

QA Flags

Automated checks flagged issues: low similarity, missing placeholders, length ratio.
